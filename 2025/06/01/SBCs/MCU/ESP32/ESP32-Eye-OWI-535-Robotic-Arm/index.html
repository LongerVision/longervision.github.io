1. Feature of OWI-535

1.1 Five DOFs of OWI-535

Refer to Pololu’s OWI-535 Robotic Arm Edge Kit:

  • a gripper
  • 120° wrist motion
  • 300° elbow motion
  • 180° shoulder motion
  • 270° base motion
